Join Us in Bridging the Digital Divide

Bandwiden is an open-source project aimed at revolutionizing content delivery and internet access worldwide. We believe in the power of collaboration to create innovative solutions that can make a real difference. While our technical development is open-source, we also critically need support in business development to ensure the widespread adoption and sustainability of our solutions.

Why Get Involved?

  • Be part of a cutting-edge project that's making the internet more accessible and efficient
  • Collaborate with a diverse team of technologists, business professionals, and social impact enthusiasts
  • Gain experience in both open-source development and technology business development
  • Make a tangible impact on communities struggling with internet access
  • Shape the future of content delivery and internet infrastructure

Technical Contributions

Our project is open-source, and we welcome contributions from developers, engineers, and technologists. Here's how you can contribute:

  • Code contributions to BandwidthBoost and CommunityCache
  • Bug reports and fixes
  • Feature suggestions and implementations
  • Documentation improvements
  • Testing and quality assurance

Visit our GitHub repository to get started: [Link to GitHub repository]

Business Development Support

We're actively seeking individuals and organizations to help drive the business side of Bandwiden. Your expertise can help us create sustainable impact. We need assistance in:

  • Partnership development with ISPs, content providers, and technology companies
  • Market research and analysis
  • Business model refinement
  • Funding and investment strategies
  • Marketing and communication planning
  • Legal and regulatory navigation
  • Project management and scaling strategies

Community Engagement

Help us connect with communities that can benefit from Bandwiden technology:

  • Identify potential pilot project locations
  • Conduct community needs assessments
  • Facilitate partnerships with local organizations
  • Develop training materials for CommunityCache users
  • Gather and share impact stories

Advocacy and Awareness

Help spread the word about Bandwiden and the importance of bridging the digital divide:

  • Write articles or blog posts about Bandwiden and its impact
  • Speak at relevant conferences or events
  • Engage with policymakers on internet access issues
  • Create educational content about the digital divide
